Each of these documents has 64 pages, one maze per page. They are meant to be printed in portrait mode. You may want to select a page range if you don't want to print all 64 pages.

  • Small mazes (20 columns)
  • Medium mazes (40 columns)
  • Large mazes (60 columns)
  • Huge mazes (80 columns)

Generate your own mazes of any size with the generator at github.com/bjacob/maze.
